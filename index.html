About Me

I'm Teri, a digital artist and animator currently studying Art & Technology at The Ohio State University. My work focuses on interactive media, 3D modeling, and animation. I explore speculative fiction themes, combining technological innovation with narrative storytelling.

Artist Statement

My art explores the intersection of digital innovation, animation, and speculative storytelling. Inspired by urban landscapes and futuristic visions, I create dynamic animations and compelling digital artworks that reimagine familiar environments into cyberpunk-infused realities. Driven by curiosity and a love for interactive technology, my work invites viewers to reconsider the possibilities of our evolving digital future.
